About Our Spotify Clone
Welcome to our Spotify Clone!
Hey there! I'm Vishnu Gupta, the creator behind this Spotify-inspired platform. With a passion for web development and a drive to understand the core concepts of HTML, CSS, and JS, I embarked on this exciting project.
Why Did We Create This?
The main motive behind creating this Spotify clone was to deepen my understanding of web development fundamentals. Through this project, I aimed to clear my concepts, refine my skills, and challenge myself to recreate a popular platform like Spotify from scratch.
How Was It Made?
Utilizing the power of HTML for structuring, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for functionality, I crafted this clone to resemble the look and feel of Spotify. While I took inspiration and guidance from YouTube tutorials and AI resources, every line of code and design choice was a learning experience for me.
